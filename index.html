Lainey Wilson

Lainey Wilson is a Country singer-songwriter artisit and actor. Wilson was just recently awarded New Artist of the Year and Female Vocalist of the Year by the CMA for 2022. She is continuing her rise as a country artisit with onnecting with her fans through social media. Wilson has really taken off with her TikTok account to connect with fans.

Early Life

Wilson is from Baskin, Lousianna and is 30 years old. From the time she was young, she performed then made her debut in 2014 in Nashville with her first album.
